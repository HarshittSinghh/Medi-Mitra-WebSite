About Our App

    Our mobile app MediMitra is a powerful mobile app designed to close the gap between policymakers and the critical data they need for making well-informed decisions. Offers a comprehensive, high-level overview of patient data, empowering better decision-making processes. Key features include:
    • Centralized data management with dedicated logins for hospital admins and super admins.
    • Hospital admins can efficiently add, update, and manage patient records in real time.
    • Super admins gain access to advanced data visualization tools, including interactive charts and custom filtering options.
    • Ensures seamless data flow from local levels to central authorities, providing a complete view of health data.
    • AI-powered chat support delivers intelligent health-related assistance and symptom guidance.
    • Integrated maps to easily locate nearby hospitals and pharmacies for enhanced patient care.
