About Our App

What MediMitra Offers:

Centralized Data Management

Dedicated logins for hospital admins and super admins to manage data centrally.

Patient Record Management

Hospital admins can efficiently add, update, and manage patient records in real time.

Data Visualization Tools

Super admins access advanced tools, including interactive charts and custom filtering options.

Seamless Data Flow

Ensures seamless data flow from local levels to central authorities for better insights.

AI-Powered Assistance

AI-powered chat support offers intelligent health-related assistance and symptom guidance.

Integrated Maps

Easily locate nearby hospitals and pharmacies for enhanced patient care.
